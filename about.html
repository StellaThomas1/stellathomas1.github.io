Stella Thomas
Stella Thomas is a global water-security strategist and systems thinker working at the intersection of water, climate, infrastructure, and finance, with a focus on strategic resilience and risk.
Global Water Fund is built on the understanding that water is a core element of human and national security—shaping food systems, energy reliability, coastal stability, migration pressures, and economic growth.
Operating at the intersection of policy, science, and capital, Global Water Fund translates water and climate risk into decision-ready intelligence and financeable frameworks.
Through a network of advisors, research collaborations, and institutional partners, the platform integrates scientific insight, geopolitical analysis, and financial structuring to anticipate systemic risk, guide intervention, and preserve long-term stability.
