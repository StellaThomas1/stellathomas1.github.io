Founder & Managing Director

Stella Thomas

Stella Thomas is a global water-security strategist and systems thinker working at the intersection of water, climate, infrastructure, finance, and geopolitical risk. Her work supports governments, investors, and operators in translating water stress into decision-ready intelligence and long-term resilience strategies.

She has advised governments, multilateral institutions, and private-sector leaders across the Middle East, Europe, Africa, and North America on water security, climate resilience, and strategic infrastructure investment.

Global Water Fund is built on the understanding that water is not a local utility issue, but a core element of human and national security — shaping food systems, energy reliability, coastal stability, migration pressures, and economic growth.

Operating as a strategic platform, Global Water Fund integrates scientific insight, geopolitical analysis, and financial structuring through a network of advisors, partners, and research collaborations to anticipate risk, guide intervention, and preserve systemic stability.
