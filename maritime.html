Maritime, Energy & Coastal Risk
Ports, shipping lanes, offshore energy, and coastal cities are among the most water-exposed assets on Earth. Drought disrupts canals. Floods shut ports. Sea-level rise threatens infrastructure. Water scarcity stresses energy systems and logistics. Global Water Fund integrates these risks into one operating picture.
